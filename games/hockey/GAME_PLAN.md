# Hockey — Game Plan

## Status

Not started.

## MVP

- Two modes: Slap Shot and Goalie
- Slap Shot: cursor aims, click timing controls shot power and direction, colored goal zones
- Goalie: puck comes in from different angles, cursor moves to intercept, click matching color to block
- Ice rink visual, puck physics (fast, low friction sliding)
- Shot/save counter per session

## Long-Term Vision

- Full rink with player movement
- Power play mode: extra time and wider goal for advantage situations
- Period-based scoring with clock
- Puck speed increases with difficulty
- Ice visual effects: skate marks, puck trail
- Analytics: shot accuracy per zone, save rate per puck angle, reaction time
