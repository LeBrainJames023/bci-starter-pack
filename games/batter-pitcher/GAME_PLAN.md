# Batter / Pitcher — Game Plan

## Status

Not started.

## MVP

- Two modes: Batter and Pitcher
- Batter: pitches come in color-coded, click the matching color within the timing window to swing
- Pitcher: choose pitch type by clicking the matching color as the ball leaves your hand
- Strike zone visualization
- Hit/miss/strike counter, batting average tracking

## Long-Term Vision

- Multiple pitch types (fastball, curve, changeup) each with different timing windows
- Power swing mechanic: click hold duration affects hit distance
- Stadium atmosphere, crowd noise on big hits
- Fielding mechanic as a third mode
- Home run derby mode: pure power, no pitching
- Analytics: hit accuracy per pitch type, timing consistency, reaction to pitch color
